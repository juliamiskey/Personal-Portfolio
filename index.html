About Me

I am the girl with the mind that never sleeps. Ever since I can remember, I’ve been coming up with ideas and projects or goals to tackle. I find a lot of satisfaction in setting a goal and seeing it through until the end. It’s how I learn and grow as a person. I am a front-end web developer with a background in events and marketing. In my work, I always strive to make complexity feel simple. I am focused on creating clean, well-structured and user friendly websites for businesses and individuals who are looking to build their online presence.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy eating spicy food, gardening, and playing sudoku
